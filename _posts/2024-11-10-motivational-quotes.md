1) An hour lost a day is an hour lost forever. - Harold Arlin

2) You can’t go back and change the beginning, but you can start where you are and change the ending. - C.S. Lewis

3) Regret for the time wasted can become a power of good in the time that remains. - Arthur Brisbane

4) The only thing standing between you and outrageous success is continuous progress. - Dan Waldschmidt

5) Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached. – Swami Vivekananda

6) Success doesn’t come from what you do occasionally. It comes from what you do consistently. – Marie Forleo

7) The best way to guarantee a loss is to quit. – Morgan Freeman

8) You are exactly where you need to be. You are not behind.

9) If you really look closely, most overnight successes took a long time. -  Steve Jobs

10) You are never too old to set another goal or to dream a new dream. – Malala Yousafzai
